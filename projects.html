Enterprise Proposal

Sandhills Biochar

Problem

Eastern red cedar is spreading across rangeland, reducing grazing capacity and increasing wildfire risk.

Opportunity

Removing cedar and converting it into biochar creates value while improving land stewardship and forage production.

Solution

Sandhills Biochar would turn invasive cedar biomass into a soil-building product that could be sold to farmers and landowners.

Revenue Potential

Possible income streams include biochar sales, cedar removal services, and future carbon-related opportunities.
