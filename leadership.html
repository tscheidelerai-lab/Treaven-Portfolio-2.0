Cover Letter to My Family

To my family and the future of our operation,

Growing up on the farm shaped who I am today. The lessons I learned working cattle, maintaining equipment, and managing land taught me responsibility, persistence, and the value of hard work. Those experiences are the reason I have chosen to pursue a career in agriculture.

Through my education at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln and my experiences with the Nebraska Tractor Test Lab, CLAAS, and international agricultural programs, I have developed technical knowledge and leadership skills that I believe can contribute meaningfully to our family operation.

My goal is not simply to join the farm but to help strengthen and grow it for the future. I want to contribute through equipment knowledge, innovation, and diversification opportunities such as Sandhills Biochar while continuing to expand our cattle operation and improve land stewardship.

I recognize that joining a family operation requires responsibility, initiative, and the ability to earn my role through hard work and commitment. My intention is to bring value to the operation while also respecting the legacy that has been built before me.

I look forward to continuing the work that our family has started and building a sustainable future for the next generation.

Sincerely,
Treaven Scheideler
